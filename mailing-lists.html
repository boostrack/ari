Mailing-lists

The Groovy mailing-lists is the main means of interaction with the Groovy developers and Groovy users.

Subscribing to the lists

Here are the public mailing lists that have been set up for the project. In order to subscribe / unsubscribe you first need to get a special link from http://xircles.codehaus.org/manage_email

Once you have got this link you can manage your subscriptions by going to the Groovy project page: http://xircles.codehaus.org/projects/groovy

Available lists

user@groovy.codehaus.orghigh volume list for questions and general discussion about Groovy
dev@groovy.codehaus.orgmedium volume list useful for those interested in ongoing developments
announce@groovy.codehaus.orgis a low volume list for announcements about new releases or major news
eclipse-plugin-user@groovy.codehaus.orglow volume list for questions and problems using the Groovy Eclipse plugin
scm@groovy.codehaus.orgmedium volume list that logs commits

Mailing-lists archive on Nabble

The mailing-lists are archived on Nabble. Below you can see the archives of the lists. In order to post messages to the mailing-lists through Nabble, you first need to be registered to the Groovy mailing-lists, as explained at the top of this page.
